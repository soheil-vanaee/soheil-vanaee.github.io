overview

The problem

Many users juggle installments, bills, and subscriptions across banking apps, SMS, and memory alone. Payments get missed not because people don't care, but because nothing pulls every commitment into one trustworthy place — especially in a right‑to‑left, Persian‑language context most finance apps ignore.

process

UX approach

I focused on designing for a right‑to‑left reading experience from the ground up, not adapting an LTR pattern after the fact.

  • Researched how people currently track bills and installments (apps, notes, memory)
  • Mapped every recurring‑payment type into one predictive calendar model
  • Wireframed RTL layouts and validated reading order and gesture direction
  • Designed a reminder system tuned to reduce notification fatigue
  • Tested high‑fidelity prototypes with Persian‑speaking users
