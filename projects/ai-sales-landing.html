process

UX approach

I worked backward from the one action that mattered — starting a trial — and built every section to remove a reason not to click.

  • Researched competing tools and how they framed their value proposition
  • Outlined a section flow: hero, problem, solution, proof, pricing, CTA
  • Designed the page in Framer with responsive breakpoints from the start
  • Wrote and tested headline variants for clarity over cleverness
  • Added social proof and pricing placement based on scroll‑depth logic
